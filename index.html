Who Am I?


I am an industrial designer with a focus on trauma informed design. I pride myself in focusing on making things that will help people who are otherwise forgotten about in design.

Socia

a Dissociation Plushie


Inspired by a traditional adolescent comfort item, Socia was designed to become a comfort tool by mirroring common grounding exercises. It contains weight for deep pressure therapy. Cotton, wool and leather offer different sensory experiences for sensory grounding. Brown coloration mimics nature, reducing stress. The form minimizes the risk of triggers associated with real animals. The pattern on the shell guides the user through the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding exercise.

Gradient Slide Light

a Sensory Friendly Dimmer


The Gradient Slide Light is a wall mounted interior lighting solution that allows for a range of atmospheres and brightnesses. Since the dimming action is caused by diffusion rather than electric fluctuations, the light does not flicker any more than standard lighting.

Habitat 1P

an Anxiety Safe Space


Habitat 1P is a deltoidal hexacontahedron made up of fifty-five identical panels. Assembled it is five feet tall. The nearly sphereical interior has no harsh corners, and feels sturdy. It has been described by people who layed inside as hammick or womb like. The internal pattern formed by the construction and wood grain gives a focal point for grounding exersize without being overwhelming. The structure is accousically isolating, which contributes to the feeling of escape.
