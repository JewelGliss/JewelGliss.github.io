Who Am I?

I am a multimedia artist and designer primarily trained in design, a self taught programmer, and hobbyist linguist. In 2017 I taught myself to code in order to create mods for my favorite rouge-like. This eventually lead to two things: my interest in esoteric programing languages which later lead to my interest in constructed languages, and being assigned as the lead programmer for a newly formed F.I.R.S.T. robotics team. Around this time, in 2018, I enrolled in a dual credit graphic design program at Jefferson College. All three of these things redirected the direction of my life. I did not consider myself an artist at the time, and I inconsitently apply that label to myself even today, but I did learn a lot about the technical side and craft of digital art and began to do freelance work. In 2020 I graduated from that program and a year later enrolled at the same school in an "Associates in Fine Arts (in Art)" degree focusing on graphic design. I learned a lot in this period of my life, such as "I don't want to spend my life doing graphic design". By 2024, with this new knowledge in hand, I graduated from that degree and enrolled at the Kansas City Art Institute for a Bachleors of Fine Arts (in Product design).
